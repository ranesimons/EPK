Rane Simons is an alt soul singer-songwriter whose music lives at the intersection of introspection and momentum. Inspired by personal transformation and emotional honesty, he crafts songs that feel both intimate and expansive—small moments woven into something larger. Blending elements of indie rock, atmospheric pop, and modern alternative, his sound is fluid, immersive, and built to resonate long after the final note.

Based in Austin, Texas, Simons embraces a deeply DIY approach to his work, remaining involved in nearly every step of the creative process. From songwriting and production to visual direction and release strategy, each project is treated as a complete world rather than a standalone track. This independence allows his music to stay unfiltered and true to lived experience, free from trend-chasing or excess polish.

At its core, Rane Simons’ music explores themes of growth, loss, resilience, and self-discovery, delivered with a vulnerability that feels immediately familiar. Whether reflecting on heartbreak or renewal, his songs invite listeners to connect, reflect, and find meaning in the spaces between. Rane Simons is building something deliberately—one honest release at a time.
